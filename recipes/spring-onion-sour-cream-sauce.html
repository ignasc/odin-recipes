Spring onion and sour cream sauce
Ingredients:
  • Spring onions
  • Sour cream
  • Dill
  • Salt (optional)
Steps:
  1. Chop spring onions into small pieces
  2. Chop dill into small pieces
  3. Mix sour cream, spring onions and dill together
  4. Add salt if needed
